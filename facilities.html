Our Facilities

At REPL, we provide state-of-the-art facilities that enhance the learning experience and support student growth in all areas. Explore some of the key facilities we offer.

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology, including digital learning tools, creating an engaging learning environment for students.

Cafeteria

The school cafeteria offers healthy and delicious meals to students and staff, ensuring that everyone is well-nourished and ready for the day ahead.

Library

Our library offers a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support students in their academic journey and foster a love for reading.

Sports Complex

We have extensive sports facilities, including a football field, basketball court, swimming pool to promote physical fitness and teamwork among students.

Auditorium

Our auditorium serves as a venue for school events, assemblies, and performances, providing students with opportunities to showcase their talents.

Science Laboratories

Our fully-equipped science labs allow students to experiment and explore biology, chemistry, and physics in a safe and hands-on learning environment.
