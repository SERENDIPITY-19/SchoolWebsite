Admission Process

Follow the steps below to complete the enrollment process:

Step 1: Application Form

Complete the online application form through our web application or visit our admissions office for a paper application.

Step 2: Submit Documents

Submit required documents, including previous academic records, birth certificate, and recent photos.

Step 3: Entrance Exam

Attend the entrance exam on the scheduled date. The exam assesses skills and readiness for our academic programs.

Step 4: Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with our admissions team and school administrators.

Step 5: Acceptance

Successful candidates will receive an acceptance letter along with the next steps for enrollment.

Admissions Criteria

Our admissions team considers a range of factors that contribute to a successful educational experience:

Academic Performance

Demonstrated readiness and performance in prior educational experiences.

Extracurricular

Participation in sports, arts, and other activities that contribute to personal growth.

Personal Motivation

Individual goals and aspirations that align with our school's mission is a plus point.

School Values Fit

Alignment with our school community values and commitment to the education.
